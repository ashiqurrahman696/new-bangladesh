Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is fostering a culture of innovation to drive economic growth and improve quality of life through technology and creative solutions.

Education

Investing in modern education systems to empower the next generation with skills for a digital and globalized world.

Sustainability

Promoting sustainable practices in all sectors to ensure a greener, cleaner, and more resilient future for all citizens.

Entrepreneurship

Encouraging entrepreneurship and supporting startups to create new opportunities and boost the national economy.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
